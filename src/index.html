Frequently Asked Questions

Find all the essential information you need in our FAQ section, designed to address the most frequently asked questions and help you make informed decisions for your child's education.

  • What are the school hours at Little Learners Academy?

    Our school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday to Friday. We also offer extended care options for parents who need early drop-off or late pick-up.

  • Is there a uniform policy for students at Little Learners Academy?

    Yes, students are required to wear a school uniform. Details about the dress code can be found in our parent handbook.

  • What extracurricular activities are available for students?

    We offer a variety of activities, including arts and crafts, music, sports, and language clubs.

  • How do you handle food allergies and dietary restrictions?

    We work closely with parents to accommodate dietary needs and ensure a safe meal environment for all students.

  • What is the teacher-to-student ratio at Little Learners Academy?

    Our small class sizes maintain an average ratio of 1 teacher per 10 students to ensure personalized attention.

  • How do you handle discipline and behavior management?

    We use positive reinforcement and a structured approach to guide behavior while promoting a respectful learning environment.

  • How do I apply for admission to Little Learners Academy?

    You can apply online through our website or visit our admissions office for assistance with the application process.

  • What safety measures are in place at Little Learners Academy?

    The safety of our students is our top priority. We have secure entry points, trained staff, regular emergency drills, and strict pick-up procedures to ensure a safe environment.
